Test 1: Weapons Categories (MediaWiki Style)

Melee Weapons Ranged Weapons Tools

Melee Weapons

Axes, bats, machetes, and sledgehammers are excellent for close combat. Good for clearing zombies in tight spaces.

Advantages: No ammunition needed, always available

Disadvantages: Requires you to be close to danger

Ranged Weapons

Pistols, shotguns, and rifles let you deal damage from a distance. Ammunition is limited, so use carefully.

Advantages: Safe distance, high damage potential

Disadvantages: Limited ammunition, loud noise

Tools

Hammers, screwdrivers, and crowbars are useful for construction and looting. Not ideal for combat.

Advantages: Versatile for other tasks

Disadvantages: Poor combat effectiveness
