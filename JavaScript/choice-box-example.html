Melee Weapons

Axes, bats, machetes, and sledgehammers are excellent for close combat. Good for clearing zombies in tight spaces.

Ranged Weapons

Pistols, shotguns, and rifles let you deal damage from a distance. Ammunition is limited, so use carefully.

Tools

Hammers, screwdrivers, and crowbars are useful for construction and looting. Not ideal for combat.
