==Event==
(Client) OnPressReloadButton

==Description==
Fires when a local player has a gun and presses the button to reload it.

==Parameters==
*player: [[IsoPlayer]] ([https://demiurgequantified.github.io/ProjectZomboidJavaDocs/zombie/characters/IsoPlayer.html JavaDoc]) - The player attempting to reload.
