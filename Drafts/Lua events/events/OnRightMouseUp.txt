==Event==
(Client) OnRightMouseUp

==Description==
Fires whenever the player releases the right mouse button, unless the input is eaten by UI.

==Parameters==
*x: [[number]] - Screen X co-ordinate of the click.
*y: [[number]] - Screen Y co-ordinate of the click.
