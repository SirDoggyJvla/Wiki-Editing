==Event==
OnHitZombie

==Description==
Fires whenever a zombie is hit by a character.

==Parameters==
*zombie: [[IsoZombie]] ([https://demiurgequantified.github.io/ProjectZomboidJavaDocs/zombie/characters/IsoZombie.html JavaDoc]) - The zombie that was hit.

*bodyPart: [[BodyPartType]] ([https://demiurgequantified.github.io/ProjectZomboidJavaDocs/zombie/characters/BodyDamage/BodyPartType.html JavaDoc]) - The type of the body part that was hit.
*weapon: [[HandWeapon]] ([https://demiurgequantified.github.io/ProjectZomboidJavaDocs/zombie/inventory/types/HandWeapon.html JavaDoc]) - The weapon the zombie was hit with.
