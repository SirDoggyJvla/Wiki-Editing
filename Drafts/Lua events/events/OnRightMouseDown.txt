==Event==
(Client) OnRightMouseDown

==Description==
Fires when the player right clicks, as long as the input isn't eaten by UI.

==Parameters==
*x: [[number]] - Screen X co-ordinate of the click.
*y: [[number]] - Screen Y co-ordinate of the click.
